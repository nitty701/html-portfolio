Top Three Movies by Nitty

The Conjuring

This is one of my all-time favorite horror movies. I have an engraved memeory of seeing this movie when it first came out. I saw it in a packed movie theatre. At that time, I had low expectations of horror movies because the selection that was coming up were horrible. There was a lot of hype to The Conjuring and it lived up to it.

Rating: 10/10
Category: Horror
Length: 1h 51mins

Click

One of the first comedy films that I fell in love with is Click with Adam Sandler. This film also inspired me to watch every other Adam Sandler movie that he was in and all the future ones. I just remember watching this film over and over and over again, and not getting tired of it. Not only was it a genuis comedic film, but it also touched my heart.

Rating: 10/10
Category: Comedy
Length: 1h 48mins

This movie came up on my Netflix reccomondations and it met my expectations for a suspenful/thriller movie. It always kept me wondering "what is going to happen next". Plus, the storyline was great.
